Interactive Live Sessions

Users can have access to specific speaker virtual sessions in real-time and will be able to host the same and share them on the platform.
  • Can host real-time/live virtual sessions.
  • Can host and track multi sessions.
  • Can host Live polling, questions/answers, chat one-to-one and one-to-many
  • Can manage speakers and attendees profile and attendance

Virtual Space Tour and booths

Virtual booths are another unique proposition of the product that provides sponsors and exhibitors with an online space to market their product or service.

  • Attendees can search, filter and sort Exhibitor & Sponsor Profiles
  • Exhibitors can host downloadable collaterals
  • Exhibitors can ensure their product marketing and upload marketing videos and images

Room for one-on-one meetings

Platform will allow the users to organise one-on-one meeting calls/chat with relevant stakeholders from the community. They can also brand their room and will have multiple access and controls.

  • Brand your room display for the meeting
  • Controls and access to reserve the room for Mentors, Investors and Sponsors
  • Allow the user to setup one-on-one meeting call & chat among the community

Breakout, Demo & Pitch rooms

User will have the ability host the event and execute multiple activity such as breakout rooms, demo, Investor pitching, meeting rooms, etc. It will allow to manage different sub-events parallel via the platform.
  • Breakout rooms create and manage with time and no of attendees.
  • Host and co-host control access can be shared
  • Demo and Pitch rooms exclusive access and visibility control
  • Different levels of access based on the sponsorship and roles

Community engagement & networking

Volta community will be on a single platform and engage into multiple activities such as group creation, individual call/chat, information on their feed board, calendar and event management, badges to the members, etc.
  • Find and interact the community members.
  • Individual call/chat and share information and messages.
  • Create and manage groups.
  • Statistics and reports to the admin about the engagement level.

Games and Team building activities

Virtual games and team building activities are the key element in today’s virtual world to keep the community engaged and also boost their rapport with internal teams and company.
  • Members can organise and play different virtual games like quiz, virtual food fest, etc.
  • Members can form teams and assign activities to boost the in-house working culture.
  • Members can share rewards and badges to winning teams.
  • Third party integration for the games and team building activities.
